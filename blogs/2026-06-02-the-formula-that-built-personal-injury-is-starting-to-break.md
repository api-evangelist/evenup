Traditional personal injury law firm growth models based on hiring more staff are becoming unsustainable due to talent shortages and accelerating retirement rates. The post presents AI as a solution to amplify top performers' impact, enabling firms to handle larger caseloads more efficiently through automation of intake, case management, and demand drafting. EvenUp argues that maintaining consistent quality standards while scaling no longer requires proportional headcount growth.
